Eli (Elizabeth) Margolin

I'm a third yeah PhD student at the University of Pennsylvania. I am broadly interested in adapting traditional security frameworks to be more privacy preserving using techniques such as Zero-Knowledge Proofs. I am advised by Sebastian Angel.

During my Master's at Duke University I was fortunate to work with Ashwin Machanavajjhala and Charles Becker . I completed my Bachelor's at Stanford.

I was previously a Security Engineer with Meta for 3 years, working in Incident Detection and Response.
